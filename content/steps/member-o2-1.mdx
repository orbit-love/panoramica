## The Path to Contribution

Now, let's assume Jeri's been meeting regularly with members for a while and
Jeri's network is strong for a Participant. What does the path to becoming a
Contributor look like?

In this situation, the Orbit Model recommends increasing _love_ since reach is already high.
To do this, we can invite Jeri to start leading workshops, and to prepare by
gaining more expertise in the community's subject matter.
